Cricket Betting FAQ

Can I bet on live cricket matches?

Yes! WowBet offers extensive live betting (in-play betting) markets where odds change dynamically ball-by-ball.

What happens to my bet if a match is abandoned?

If a match is abandoned due to rain or other reasons without an official result, all bets on the match winner are usually voided and your stake is refunded. However, bets on completed markets (e.g., Top Batsman if the innings was finished) may still stand.
