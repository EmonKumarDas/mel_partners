How to Play Gates of Olympus

Gates of Olympus uses a 6x5 grid and the popular "Pay Anywhere" system. You win by landing 8 or more identical symbols on the screen, triggering the Tumble feature where winning symbols disappear and are replaced by new ones falling from above.

Zeus Multipliers: At any time during the base game or free spins, Zeus (standing to the right of the reels) may hurl a winged orb onto the grid. These orbs carry multipliers ranging from 2x up to an incredible 500x. If a tumble sequence results in a win and there are multipliers on screen, their values are added together and applied to the total win.

The Free Spins Feature

Landing 4 or more Zeus Scatter symbols triggers the Free Spins round with 15 initial spins.

This is where the game's high volatility truly shines. During Free Spins, whenever a multiplier orb hits and results in a win, that multiplier value is added to a global multiplier meter. For the rest of the free spins round, whenever a new multiplier orb lands on a winning spin, the total accumulated global multiplier is applied to that win!
