How to Play Sweet Bonanza

Sweet Bonanza operates on a "Pay Anywhere" mechanic on a 6x5 grid. Unlike traditional slots, you don't need symbols to line up on specific paylines. Instead, you win by landing 8 or more identical symbols anywhere on the screen.

The Tumble Feature: Whenever you hit a winning combination, the winning symbols disappear, and new symbols drop from above to fill the empty spaces. This continues as long as new winning combinations are formed.

The Free Spins Feature

The real excitement in Sweet Bonanza lies in the Free Spins round. Landing 4 or more Lollipop Scatters anywhere on the reels triggers 10 Free Spins.

During the Free Spins, look out for the Rainbow Bomb Multiplier symbols. These stay on the screen until the end of the tumbling sequence and carry a random multiplier value from 2x up to an incredible 100x. At the end of a tumble, the values of all bombs on the screen are added together and multiplied by the total win of that sequence.
