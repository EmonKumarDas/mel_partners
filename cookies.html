Cookies Policy

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences, track referrals, and improve your experience.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core features like security and account access.

Analytics Cookies

We use analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with our website. This helps us improve our services and content.

Tracking Cookies

These cookies are essential for our affiliate program. They track referrals from your marketing channels to ensure you receive proper credit and commissions.

Preference Cookies

These cookies remember your settings and preferences, such as language selection, to provide a personalized experience.

3. Cookie Duration

Our affiliate tracking cookies typically last for 30 days, ensuring you receive credit for referrals within that period. Other cookies may expire when you close your browser or persist for up to 1 year.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services (such as Google Analytics) that place cookies on your device. These services have their own privacy policies.

5. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website and affiliate tracking.

6. Your Consent

By using our website, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy. You can withdraw consent at any time by adjusting your browser settings.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookies Policy periodically. Please check back for any changes.

8. Contact Us

For questions about our cookie practices, contact us at partners@melbetaffiliate.com
